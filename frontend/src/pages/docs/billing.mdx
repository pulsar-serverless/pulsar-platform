# Billing and Pricing Plans

## Introduction

Web App Pulsar offers flexible pricing plans to meet your needs. You can choose between the Free Plan and the Pro Plan based on your requirements. This document will guide you through the features of each plan and how to change your pricing plan.

## Pricing Plans Overview

### Free Plan

The Free Plan is ideal for experimenting, learning, and building small-scale serverless applications. It includes the following features:

- **Bandwidth**: 1 GB per month for outbound data transfer
- **Memory**: 1 GB per month of dedicated function memory
- **Requests**: 100 invocations per month

<Callout>
	The Free Plan is perfect for developers learning to build serverless
	applications, small-scale projects with low traffic, and testing and
	development environments.
</Callout>

### Pro Plan

The Pro Plan offers more resources, allowing you to handle higher traffic and more complex applications. It includes the following features:

- **Bandwidth**: 10 GB per month for outbound data transfer
- **Memory**: 10 GB per month of dedicated function memory
- **Requests**: 500 invocations per month

<Callout>
	The Pro Plan is suitable for production applications with moderate to high
	traffic, projects requiring more memory and bandwidth, and complex serverless
	applications.
</Callout>

## Changing Pricing Plans

To change your pricing plan, follow these steps:

<Steps>
### Step 1

Go to the project page and navigate to the specific project for which you want to change the pricing plan. Click on the "Free Plan" button.

<Image
	src="/images/project-free-plan.png"
	alt="Project Free Plan Button"
	width={500}
	height={500}
/>

### Step 2

The "Change Pricing Plan" modal will appear. In this modal, you can see the available options: Free Plan and Pro Plan. Click on the "Choose Plan" button for the desired plan.

<Image
	src="/images/pricing-change.png"
	alt="Change Pricing Plan Modal"
	width={500}
	height={500}
/>

### Step 3

A confirmation dialog will appear, indicating that you are about to change your serverless hosting plan. Review the details and click "Confirm" to proceed with the change.

<Callout>
	"Changing your serverless plan may affect costs & resource limits. Review
	details & confirm."
</Callout>

<Image
	src="/images/pricing-confirm.png"
	alt="Confirmation Dialog"
	width={500}
	height={500}
/>

### Step 4

Once you click "Confirm," a message will appear confirming that your project pricing plan has been changed.

<Image src="/images/pricing-changed.png" alt="Pricing Plan Changed Message" width={500} height={500} />
</Steps>

## Conclusion

Web App Pulsar's flexible pricing plans allow you to choose the best option for your application, whether you're just getting started or managing a high-traffic production app. Make sure to review your needs and select the plan that provides the resources you require.
