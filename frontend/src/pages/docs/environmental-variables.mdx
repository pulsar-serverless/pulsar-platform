# How to configure your environmental variables
<Steps>

### Step 1

First go to a project and click on the `environmental variables` tab.Then you will be presented with environmental variables setting page
<Image src="/images/env-variables.png" alt="Hello" width={500} height={500} />

### Step 2

Then add your environmental variable by clicking `Add another`.
<Image src="/images/env-variables.png" alt="Hello" width={500} height={500} />

### Step 3

Next add your environmental variables as key-value pair and click `Save`
<Image src="/images/add-env-variable.png" alt="Hello" width={500} height={500} />


Congratulations! you have successfully set up environmental variables for your project.
</Steps>
